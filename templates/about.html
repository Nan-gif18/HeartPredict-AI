About the Heart Disease Prediction Tool

How it Works

This application uses machine learning to predict the likelihood of heart disease based on various clinical parameters. The model was trained on a dataset of patients with and without heart disease.

Model Information

The prediction model uses a Random Forest classifier, which was selected after evaluating multiple machine learning algorithms including Decision Trees, Logistic Regression, and K-Nearest Neighbors. The Random Forest model was chosen due to its superior performance in terms of accuracy, precision, and recall.
